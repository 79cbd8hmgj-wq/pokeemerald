==============================================================================
FINAL TARGETED POLISH PASS (V6)
==============================================================================

Focused role-definition and level-up polish applied to the last major cleanup targets:
- Jigglypuff
- Wigglytuff
- Paras
- Parasect
- Noctowl
- Spinarak
- Ariados

Main changes:
- Jigglypuff now reaches Dream Eater naturally so its sleep-disruption role is less TM-dependent.
- Wigglytuff now has a clearer Psychic/Fighting progression through Bulk Up and Brick Break in level-up.
- Paras and Parasect now show their Bug/Ghost identity earlier with Astonish and later with Giga Drain support.
- Noctowl now gets Will-O-Wisp by level-up to better support its Fire/Psychic control role.
- Spinarak and Ariados now have a more complete ambush-trapper water progression with Rain Dance and Hydro Pump.

This pass is intended as final learnset-role cleanup before any additional broad balance work.


==============================================================================
AGGRESSIVE BST REBALANCE PASS (V7)
==============================================================================

This pass expands beyond the earlier narrow watchlist and applies broader BST support
to underpowered final-stage or stand-alone Pokemon that still lagged behind the new
power baseline. The goal is not to make all of them top-tier, but to stop them from

V10 REDISTRIBUTION PASS NOTES
==============================================================================

This pass focused on redistributing existing BST more intelligently instead of primarily raising or lowering totals.

Redistribution targets
- Blastoise: shifted toward slower mixed bulk/tank identity for Water / Ground
- Raichu: reduced pure speed skew and added sturdier Electric / Steel profile
- Donphan: pushed harder into physical wall / bruiser stats
- Sceptile: redistributed into a more balanced fast mixed Dragon-style attacker
- Milotic: reduced excess speed and improved bulky special dragon identity
- Paras / Parasect: reinforced slow disruptive Bug / Ghost stat lines with more special bulk
- Hoothoot / Noctowl: better matched Fire / Psychic special attacker / utility roles
- Spinarak / Ariados: smoothed awkward stat lines into clearer ambush bruiser roles
- Sunkern: kept weak but gave it a cleaner special growth profile
- Jigglypuff / Wigglytuff: reduced awkward stat waste and pushed clearer Psychic and Psychic / Fighting roles
- Delibird: moved away from over-reliance on Speed into a more functional offensive utility spread
- Luvdisc: lowered excessive speed concentration and improved special utility profile

This pass intentionally emphasized role definition over raw BST movement.

- Keep BST stable
