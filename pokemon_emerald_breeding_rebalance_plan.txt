POKÉMON EMERALD BREEDING REBALANCE PLAN
Merged Water Egg Group + Expanded Dual Egg Group System
Based on current Emerald egg group reference and the uploaded Gen 1-3 catalog

======================================================================
1) DESIGN GOALS
======================================================================

Primary goals:
- Remove unnecessary breeding friction
- Make aquatic breeding intuitive
- Increase the number of useful bridge species
- Give weaker or lower-value Pokémon more breeding utility
- Preserve some family identity so breeding does not become meaningless

Core rule changes:
- Merge Water 1, Water 2, and Water 3 into one Egg Group: WATER
- Keep DITTO unchanged
- Keep UNDISCOVERED unchanged
- Increase the number of dual Egg Group Pokémon substantially
- Prefer changes that feel natural by body plan, ecology, or lore

Target feel:
- Chain breeding should still exist, but not feel annoying
- More lines should be able to pass useful Egg Moves without obscure routes
- Water species should no longer feel arbitrarily split apart
- A noticeable share of weak or awkward Pokémon should gain value as bridge breeders

Suggested target:
- Roughly 40% to 55% of breedable Pokémon should end up dual Egg Group

======================================================================

======================================================================

Keep these groups:

- Water 1
- Water 2
- Water 3

Replacement:
- All three become WATER

======================================================================
3) HIGH-LEVEL BALANCE RULES
======================================================================

Use these rules when assigning second Egg Groups:

A. Add a second Egg Group if:
- the species clearly fits two biological/archetypal categories
- the change creates better breeding routes
- the line is weak or awkward and could use utility
- the change supports Egg Move accessibility
- the change helps connect isolated move pools

B. Avoid adding second Egg Groups if:
- the result feels absurd or breaks theme badly
- the species would become too universal a bridge
- the change collapses too many families together
- the line is already a top-tier bridge species and needs no help

C. Be cautious with these groups:
- Dragon
- Human-Like
- Monster
- Field

These groups are extremely powerful if spread too broadly.

F. WATER + FIELD
Use for semi-aquatic beasts, amphibians, otter/seal/whale-like lines, and practical bridge breeders.

- Duskull line

======================================================================
9) CHANGES TO AVOID EARLY
======================================================================

Avoid these until after testing:

- Making too many species Dragon
- Giving Human-Like to broad mammal groups
- Converting most Flying lines into universal Field bridges
- Giving Monster to too many physically bulky lines
- Turning rare identities into generic compatibility hubs

Specific caution examples:
- Salamence line does not need more flexibility
- Bagon line is already powerful enough thematically
- Beldum line should not become breedable unless you want a major rule rewrite
- Fossils should stay somewhat special unless your whole mod pushes toward openness

======================================================================
10) TESTING CHECKLIST
======================================================================

After implementing the first pass, test these questions:

1. Can players now get useful Egg Moves without absurd chains?
2. Do Water species feel more intuitive?
3. Are weak species gaining breeding value?
4. Are Dragon routes still somewhat special?
5. Are Human-Like routes still distinct?
6. Did any one group become too universal?
7. Are there still a few meaningful family identities left?

Watch especially for:
- too-easy Dragon move spread
- too many Field-based routes trivializing everything
- one or two lines becoming mandatory super-bridges

======================================================================
12) FINAL RECOMMENDATION
======================================================================

Best first version:
- Merge all Water groups
- Expand dual Egg Groups mainly in Field/Fairy, Grass/Fairy, Water/Field, Water/Amorphous, Flying/Field, and Bug/Amorphous
- Use Dragon sparingly
- Use Human-Like sparingly
- Give weaker lines extra breeding value before strong lines

This version should make Emerald breeding feel much more open without turning the system into total chaos.
